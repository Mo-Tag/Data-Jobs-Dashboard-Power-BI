## Introduction
This dashboard was created for **Job Seekers, Job Transitioners, and Job Swappers** to solve a common problem: information about the data job market is often scattered and difficult to grasp. Using a *real-world dataset of 2024 data science job postings* (including titles, salaries, and locations), this project provides a single, easy-to-use interface to explore market trends and compensation.

- **Top Roles**: Identification of the most in-demand titles (e.g., Data Scientist, Senior Data Analyst).

- **Salary Benchmarking**: Clear visibility into median salaries across different roles and experience levels.

- **Global Distribution**: Mapping where the highest concentration of data opportunities exists.
